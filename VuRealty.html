Vu Properties

About

Vu Properties is a realty investment firm founded by a father and son team: Thang and Michael Vu. Thang is originally a computer engineer who left the industry in 2002 and moved to Florida to invest in real estate. Michael is a certified public accountant who had hands on training in property managment while helping his father manage his properties.

Experience

Michael and Thang have close to 20 years of combined experience in real estate and hold real estate licenses. They have weathered the real estate recession in the early 2010's and rode the real estate bull market since then. Both are experts in property search, contract interpretation, aquisition, and property management.

Portfolio

The firm has a combined 2 commercial/retail buildings and 10+ residential homes that produce monthly income. All properties are concentrated in the greater St. Petersburg area.
